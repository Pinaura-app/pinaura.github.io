Pinaura

Support

For help with Pinaura, include your Windows version, Pinaura version, capture mode, and whether OCR, recording, or library indexing was involved.

Before contacting support

  • Restart Pinaura from the tray menu.
  • Confirm you are using the latest Microsoft Store version.
  • Open Settings > Performance and copy the diagnostic summary.
  • For OCR issues, confirm Windows has at least one OCR language installed.

Local logs

In Pinaura, open Settings > Performance to open the data folder, logs folder, or copy diagnostics.

Store purchases

Microsoft Store manages Store installs, updates, purchases, and refunds.
