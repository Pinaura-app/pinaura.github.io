Pinaura

Documentation

Quick start

Use the tray icon or capture shortcut to capture a region, window, full screen, delayed capture, scrolling window, or full web page.

Pin and annotate

Captured screenshots open as floating windows. Use the toolbar for highlights, pen strokes, text, arrows, shapes, callouts, pixelation, crop, OCR selection, canvas expansion, and retake area adjustment.

Library

The local library stores screenshots and recordings with titles, tags, favorites, OCR text, metadata, filters, duplicate detection, and retention cleanup controls.

Privacy

Screenshots, OCR text, recordings, and metadata stay local unless you export or share them.
